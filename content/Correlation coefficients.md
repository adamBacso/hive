$$\text{correlation coefficient }r=\frac{\text{covariance of x and y}}{\sqrt{ (\text{variance of x}) }\sqrt{ (\text{variance of y}) }}$$
$r=+1$ → perfect **positive** correlation 
$r=0$ → no linear correlation 
$r=-1$ → perfect **negative** correlation

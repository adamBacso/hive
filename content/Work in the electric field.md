$$W_{\text{by field}}=\vec{F}\cdot \vec{s}=q\cdot \vec{E}\cdot \vec{s}$$
>[!info] Voltage
$V_{AB}$: potential difference between **A** and **B** ($\vec{E}\cdot \vec{s}$)
$$W=q\cdot V_{AB}$$
$$V_{AB}=\frac{W}{q}$$
$[V_{AB}]=\frac{J}{C}=1V$ (volt)
> >other term is *voltage*

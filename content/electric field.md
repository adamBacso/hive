# Electric field 
*elektromos térerősség*
>a quantity to characterize electric fields 

$\vec{E}=\frac{\vec{F}}{q}$
$[\vec{E}]=\frac{N}{C}(=\frac{V}{m})$

>[!info] Electric field lines
>can characterize both direction and magnitude

- inkjet printers 
- electric desooting (*koromleválasztás*)

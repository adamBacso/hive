>[!note] Missing wavelength
>$f=200Hz$
>$c=340Hz$
>$\lambda=?$
>$$c=\lambda\cdot f$$
>$$\lambda=\frac{c}{f}=>\frac{340Hz}{200Hz}$$
>$$\lambda=1.7m$$

>[!note] Missing period
>What is the period of red light?
>$\lambda=700nm=700\cdot 10^{-9}m$
>($c=3\cdot 10^8m/s$)
>$T=?$
>$$c=\frac{\lambda}{T}$$
>$$T=\frac{\lambda}{c}=>\frac{700\cdot 10^{-9}m}{3\cdot 10^8m/s}$$
>$$T=2.33\cdot 10^{-15}s$$

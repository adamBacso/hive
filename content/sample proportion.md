# with attribute
$$\hat{p}=\frac{1}{n}\sum^{n}_{i=1}X_{i}$$
$n=\text{sample size}$
$\sum^{n}_{i=1}X_{i}=\text{sum of indicator variables}$
$X_{i=}\begin{cases}1\text{ : if sample member }i\text{ has the attribute}\\0\text{ : otherwise}\end{cases}$

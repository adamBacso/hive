# Three components
1. lungs
2. [[vocal folds]] (within [[larynx]])
3. [[articulators]] 

# Voice type 
affected by the size of the [[vocal folds|vocal cords]] and [[vocal tract]] 
>**larger** vocal fold results in a **deeper** voice
>**shorter** vocal fold results in a **higher** voice

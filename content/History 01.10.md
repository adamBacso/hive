# → Tripartition
- Habsburgs: Royal Hungary/Hungarian Kingdom
- Turkish Occupied Hungary
- Transylvanian Principality → vassal state of Ottomans

![](https://hungarynews.files.wordpress.com/2013/12/hungary-in-three.jpg)
